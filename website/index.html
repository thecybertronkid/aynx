Trust & Performance

Answers to Your Common Questions

Everything you need to know about accounts, privacy, PC performance, and clean uninstallation.

Do I Need an Account?

No, downloading is fully functional without any account. Google Sign-In is strictly optional and only required to activate your free 30-day Plus trial, sync settings to the cloud, or restore purchases. You will never be forced to register.

No Ads. No Tracking.

AYNX is supported entirely by direct, optional subscriptions. We display no advertising banners, no popups, no cryptocurrency miners, and do not track or sell your download logs. Privacy is built into our core framework.

Can I Uninstall It Cleanly?

Yes. AYNX installs cleanly and does not clutter your Windows registry or leave silent background processes running. When uninstalled using standard Windows Settings, all files are completely removed.
